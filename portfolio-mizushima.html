Portfolio Project: Mizushima

Description

  • Mr. Mizushima

  • SPLUS Software

Mizushima is a series of mobile projects, encompassing both new development and maintenance.

Within this series, the VR project was a new development initiative, aimed at creating a VR effect from imported and captured videos/pictures. My role in this project involved translating between Japanese and Vietnamese, communicating with the client, analyzing the business requirements, and ensuring that developers met task deadlines.

In addition to the VR project, there were several maintenance projects within the chain. I was responsible for translation, executing tests, and assisting the project manager with team management.
